These next two plots do a good job at aggregating a song's performance over the course of the whole year. In the boxplots, you'll be able to see minimum, maximum, median, and Q1/Q3 quartiles. These simple statistics generally shows where a song ranked during the time they were on the charts. A large Q1, Q3 difference means that the song climbed up (or down) the chart, while a small difference means the song stayed in a relatively consistent spot.
