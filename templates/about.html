Introduction

With the advent of streaming services like Spotify and Apple Music, the music landscape has evolved into a dynamic and fast-paced environment. Songs can become hit records across the country within a matter of a couple days. And a few days days later another song can replace a previously reigning one. In this project, I use Spotify's daily Top 200 charts to explore the journey of a song's rank. Hopefully through the visualizations, you will be able to see how well a Top 200 song did during the year 2017. Some songs like "rockstar" by Post Malone had a strong and consistent run, while songs like "All I Want for Christmas Is You" by Mariah Carey saw strong rankings for only a couple days during Christmas time. The Story

The goal of this project is to explore the dynamic nature of songs streamed from Spotify. Using the visualizations, you can compare how "hot" a song was with other songs. Because everyone's music taste is different, the project lets the user choose the songs they're interested in analyzing.
